# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered experiences and interfaces that are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with product goals. They collaborate closely with Product Managers to understand user needs and with Developers to ensure technical feasibility.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Design wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Define user interface standards and design systems
- Collaborate with Product Managers on user requirements and acceptance criteria
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Document design specifications and interaction patterns

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Reduce friction in user workflows and decision-making
- Maintain design consistency and brand alignment
- Enable rapid iteration based on user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions with cross-functional teams
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Design system documentation and component libraries
- Weekly collaboration with Product and Development teams

### Cross-Functional Interactions
- **With Product Managers**: Align on user needs, business goals, and success metrics
- **With Developers**: Ensure designs are implementable and provide detailed specifications
- **With QA**: Participate in usability testing and acceptance criteria validation

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers design, build, and maintain the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and automation tooling that enable rapid, reliable software delivery. They bridge operations and development to optimize deployment efficiency and system reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and manage cloud infrastructure and environments
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Automate testing, building, and deployment processes
- Monitor system performance, availability, and security
- Manage infrastructure as code and deployment automation
- Collaborate with Developers on testing automation and deployment strategies
- Support incident response and post-mortem activities

### Goals
- Enable rapid, safe deployments with minimal manual intervention
- Maintain high system reliability, performance, and security
- Reduce cycle time from code commit to production
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and rollbacks

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and pipeline documentation
- Deployment status reports and incident updates
- Collaboration with Developers on test automation and CI/CD improvements
- Infrastructure architecture reviews and optimization discussions

### Cross-Functional Interactions
- **With Developers**: Enable automation, provide pipeline feedback, and support test environments
- **With QA**: Coordinate test automation and staging environment management
- **With Security Lead**: Ensure security scanning in pipelines and infrastructure hardening
- **With Release Manager**: Coordinate deployment orchestration and rollback procedures

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers own the planning, coordination, and orchestration of software releases. They ensure all stakeholders are aligned, readiness criteria are met, and communication is clear throughout the release lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain release plans and timelines
- Coordinate cross-team readiness (development, QA, ops, documentation)
- Verify all acceptance criteria and quality gates are met before deployment
- Manage release notes, documentation, and communication to stakeholders
- Orchestrate deployment activities and manage rollback procedures
- Track and communicate release status and risks
- Conduct post-release verification and support incident response

### Goals
- Deliver releases on schedule with minimal risk and incidents
- Ensure clear communication and alignment across all stakeholders
- Enable rapid rollback and incident recovery if needed
- Maintain transparency on release readiness and deployment status

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness checklists and sign-offs
- Release notes and deployment communications
- Stakeholder status updates and risk reports
- Deployment coordination meetings and post-release retrospectives

### Cross-Functional Interactions
- **With Product Managers**: Align on feature completion and business priorities
- **With Developers**: Verify code completion and technical readiness
- **With QA**: Confirm quality gates and acceptance criteria validation
- **With DevOps Engineers**: Coordinate deployment execution and rollback procedures
- **With Project Managers**: Track timeline adherence and escalate delays

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure that security and compliance requirements are integrated throughout the project lifecycle. They conduct threat modeling, code reviews, and security testing to minimize risk and maintain organizational security standards.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct threat modeling and security architecture reviews
- Define security requirements and acceptance criteria
- Perform or coordinate security code reviews and penetration testing
- Manage security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
- Identify and track security vulnerabilities and remediation
- Collaborate with teams on secure coding practices and compliance
- Support incident response for security-related issues
- Maintain security documentation and compliance artifacts

### Goals
- Proactively identify and mitigate security risks
- Ensure compliance with organizational and regulatory security standards
- Foster a security-aware culture across the team
- Enable rapid response to security incidents and vulnerabilities

### Typical Communication
- Security requirement specifications and threat models
- Security scan reports and vulnerability assessments
- Code review comments focused on security best practices
- Compliance documentation and audit reports
- Security incident communications and remediation plans

### Cross-Functional Interactions
- **With Developers**: Review code for security issues and provide secure coding guidance
- **With Product Managers**: Define security requirements for features and products
- **With DevOps Engineers**: Ensure security scanning in pipelines and infrastructure hardening
- **With Release Manager**: Verify security readiness before releases

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical implementation by gathering, analyzing, and translating requirements. They work closely with stakeholders, Product Managers, and technical teams to ensure alignment and reduce miscommunication.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and analyze business requirements from stakeholders
- Translate business needs into actionable technical specifications
- Document use cases, workflows, and acceptance criteria
- Identify dependencies, gaps, and risks in requirements
- Participate in backlog refinement and prioritization
- Validate solutions against original business objectives
- Create process documentation and training materials
- Support stakeholder communication and expectation management

### Goals
- Ensure clear alignment between business needs and technical delivery
- Reduce rework and scope creep through thorough requirements analysis
- Enable faster decision-making with complete, accurate information
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction through effective communication

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and specification sheets
- Process flows, use cases, and swimlane diagrams
- Meeting notes and decision logs
- Stakeholder impact assessments and change request analysis

### Cross-Functional Interactions
- **With Product Managers**: Collaborate on requirement definition and prioritization
- **With Project Managers**: Support timeline estimation and risk identification
- **With Developers**: Clarify technical feasibility and provide detailed specifications
- **With Stakeholders**: Gather requirements and communicate progress updates
